About Me

Senior Android Engineer and Mobile Architect with 10+ years building large-scale, production-ready mobile platforms across ride-hailing, fintech, logistics, food delivery, e-learning, and on-demand services.

Currently Team Lead & Senior Android Developer at Grepix Infotech, where I design and deliver complex multi-app ecosystems (Customer, Driver, Merchant, Admin) used across multiple regions and languages.

I specialize in: ride-hailing systems (bidding, pooling, recurring rides, toll automation, red zones); fintech & payment platforms (wallets, bank transfers, QR payments); high-scale Android architectures with MVVM, Jetpack, and Compose; secure payment integrations and encrypted API communication; and location-heavy apps with real-time tracking and route optimization.

Previously contributed to Afghanistan’s first digital payment platform (HesabPay) and co-founded the e-learning platform Maktab, which scaled to 30,000+ active users before suspension in 2025 due to financial constraints.

I enjoy solving hard problems involving scalability, payments, real-time systems, and complex business logic. I’ve worked across Android, iOS, Desktop, and Web, which allows me to design systems with a strong cross-platform mindset. Open to senior engineering, lead, and architecture-focused roles.
