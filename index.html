About

Senior Android Engineer and Mobile Architect with 10+ years building large-scale, production-grade platforms across ride-hailing, fintech, logistics, food delivery, e-learning, and on-demand services.

Currently Acting Team Lead at Grepix Infotech, leading Android architecture and development for multi-app ecosystems (Customer, Driver, Merchant, Admin) deployed across multiple regions with localization and theming support.

Core expertise includes ride-hailing platforms (bidding, pooling, recurring rides, toll automation, red zones, gender preferences); fintech & digital payments (wallets, bank transfers, QR payments, prepaid & postpaid flows); scalable Android architectures using MVVM, Jetpack, and Jetpack Compose; secure API communication with encryption and payment gateway integrations; and location-heavy systems with real-time tracking and advanced route analysis.

Previously worked on Afghanistan’s first online payment platform (HesabPay) and co-founded the e-learning product Maktab, which scaled to 30,000+ active users before suspension in 2025 due to financial constraints.

Hands-on experience across Android, iOS, Desktop, and Web, with a system-level, cross-platform mindset. Open to senior, lead, staff, and architecture-focused roles.
